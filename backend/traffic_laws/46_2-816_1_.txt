Title: ยง 46.2-816.1. Careless driving and infliction of injury or death on vulnerable road users; penalty.
Content:
A. As used in this section, "vulnerable road user" means a pedestrian; the operator of or passenger on a bicycle, electric personal assistive mobility device, electric power-assisted bicycle, wheel chair or wheel chair conveyance, skateboard, roller skates, motorized skateboard or scooter, or animal-drawn vehicle or any attached device; or any person riding an animal.
B. It is a Class 1 misdemeanor to operate a motor vehicle in a careless or distracted manner such that the careless or distracted operation is the proximate cause of serious bodily injury as defined in ยง 18.2-51.4 to or the death of a vulnerable road user who is lawfully present on the highway at the time of injury or death.
C. A prosecution or proceeding under ยง 46.2-852 is a bar to a prosecution or proceeding under this section for the same act, and a prosecution or proceeding under this section is a bar to a prosecution or proceeding under ยง 46.2-852 for the same act.

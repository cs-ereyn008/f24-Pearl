Title: ยง 46.2-813. Occupation of trailer being towed on highways.
Content:
No person shall occupy a house trailer or camping trailer while it is being towed on a public highway in this Commonwealth. No operator of a towing vehicle shall knowingly permit another person to occupy a house trailer or camping trailer as defined in ยง 46.2-100 while it is being towed.
In any civil proceeding, the violation of this section shall not constitute negligence per se.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • What is Traffic Tamer?

    Traffic Tamer is a web application designed to help users easily navigate and understand traffic laws. It provides automated law lookups, simplified explanations, and up-to-date traffic law information based on location.

  • How does Traffic Tamer work?

    By utilizing traffic data and machine learning algorithms, Traffic Tamer categorizes and presents relevant traffic laws based on the user's state, county, local police code, and violation code. It simplifies complex laws into layman's terms to enhance comprehension.

  • Who can benefit from Traffic Tamer?

    Traffic Tamer is beneficial for general drivers, traffic violators, law students specializing in traffic law, and driver improvement class students. It also serves legal professionals, insurance agents, and policymakers.

  • Is Traffic Tamer updated with new traffic laws?

    Yes, Traffic Tamer stays up to date with current traffic laws, ensuring users have access to the latest regulations and can find relevant information based on their location.

  • What platforms does Traffic Tamer run on?

    Traffic Tamer runs on a Linux system with Apache as the web server, managing data with MySQL and SQLite. The front end is built using React, and it is containerized using Docker for deployment.

  • How can I access Traffic Tamer?

    Traffic Tamer is accessible online through any web browser, providing a user-friendly interface to help users quickly find and understand traffic laws.
